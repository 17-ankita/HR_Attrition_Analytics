# HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end HR analytics dashboard built using **Python, SQL, Streamlit, Pandas, and Plotly** to analyze employee attrition patterns, workforce trends, employee satisfaction, workload impact, and retention risk factors.

## Live Demo

https://hr-attrition-analytics-1234.streamlit.app/

## Project Overview
The HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard helps analyze employee turnover and identify key factors that may influence attrition. It provides interactive insights into department-wise attrition, job role trends, overtime impact, income patterns, satisfaction levels, and employee segmentation.

This project combines:
- Data cleaning
- Exploratory data analysis
- SQL-based analysis
- KPI tracking
- Interactive dashboarding
- HR business intelligence

## Features
- Interactive Streamlit dashboard
- Dynamic sidebar filters
- KPI cards for HR metrics
- Department-wise attrition analysis
- Job role-wise attrition analysis
- Overtime impact analysis
- Age group and income band analysis
- Satisfaction and work-life balance insights
- SQL-style business insights
- Department × Job Role heatmap
- Dataset explorer

## Dashboard Modules

### 1. Overview
- Total employees
- Employees left
- Active employees
- Attrition rate
- Average age
- Average monthly income
- Average years at company

### 2. Department Analysis
- Department-wise attrition rate
- Average income by department
- Department scorecard
- Job role-wise attrition rate

### 3. Employee Segments
- Attrition by age group
- Attrition by income band
- Attrition by gender
- Attrition by marital status

### 4. Satisfaction & Workload
- Overtime impact on attrition
- Business travel impact
- Job satisfaction trends
- Work-life balance trends

### 5. SQL Insights
- Top job roles by attrition rate
- Department-level workforce summary
- Department × Job Role attrition heatmap
